GTA 5 (also known as: GTA V / Grand Theft Auto V / Grand Theft Auto Five) is the fifth numbered game in the Grand Theft Auto series from Rockstar Games. It is the 15th overall title in the iconic Grand Theft Auto franchise. GTA 5 was released on both PS3 and Xbox 360 on September 17, 2013. GTA Online, (also known as GTAO) the multiplayer section of GTA 5, launched on October 1, 2013. GTA 5 was released on PlayStation 4 and Xbox One with significant changes on November 18, 2014, and on PC on April 14, 2015. Support for GTA Online continues with frequent updates.
